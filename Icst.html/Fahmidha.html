About ICST

ICST is a leading private institution in Sri Lanka offering globally recognized diplomas and degrees. Our campus is equipped with modern facilities and a student-friendly environment. ICST stands for Innovative College of Science and Technology, located in Sri Lanka. It offers diploma, certificate, and degree programs in areas like: - Information Technology - Business Management - Engineering - English Language
